# Good Luck Potato (Minecraft 1.21.8)
Adds a new potato, a very realistic potato! But that’s just the start—now you can craft a **Pocket-Sized Particle Accelerator** on its own, and then transform it into a **Realism Enhancer** that cranks every detail in your world up to insane levels. From hyper-immersive textures to lifelike lighting and physics tweaks, everything feels more real than ever, **giving your game an oddly satisfying touch of reality that’s powered entirely by science… not potatoes.**

### **Requires Fabric API**
